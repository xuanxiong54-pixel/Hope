# Anchor of Hope - Disaster Psychological Reconstruction Platform

## Project Overview

"Anchor of Hope" is a static website platform for disaster psychological reconstruction based on positive psychology theories. The platform deeply integrates hope theory, learned optimism theory, and emotional narrative theory to provide structured psychological support for users experiencing disasters or traumatic events.

## Design Philosophy

### Core Visual Language
- **Warm and Healing**: Using soft cream, coral orange, and sage green as primary colors
- **Professional and Trustworthy**: Combination of Noto Serif SC and Noto Sans SC fonts to demonstrate professionalism
- **Hope and Upward**: Conveying positive energy through gradient colors and upward visual elements
- **Cultural Integration**: Reflecting Eastern collectivist values and "we are together" philosophy

### Color Scheme
- **Main Background**: Warm cream (#F8F6F0)
- **Accent Colors**: Soft coral orange (#E8A598) and light sage green (#B8C5A6)
- **Text Color**: Deep charcoal (#2C3E50) ensuring 4.5:1 contrast ratio
- **Secondary Colors**: Warm beige (#F2E7D5) and light gray-blue (#D6E2E8)

## Functional Modules

### 1. Homepage (index.html)
- **Welcome & Assessment**: Warm greetings and preliminary psychological state assessment
- **Core Features Display**: Introduction and entry points for three core modules
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual hope energy bar and achievement statistics
- **Responsive Design**: Perfectly adapted for mobile and desktop

### 2. Hope Path Navigation (path-navigation.html)
- **Goal Setter**: Guide users to set specific, measurable small goals
- **Path Generator**: Generate 3 optional action paths for each goal
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual progress display and milestone celebrations
- **Energy System**: Hope energy bar and achievement unlocking mechanism

### 3. Cognitive Restructuring Lab (cognitive-lab.html)
- **ABCDE Model**: Complete cognitive restructuring process
  - A (Adversity): Record difficult situations
  - B (Belief): Identify automatic thoughts
  - C (Consequence): Analyze emotional consequences
  - D (Disputation): Evidence debate session
  - E (Energization): Positive restructuring results
- **Thought Recording**: Structured thought capture and analysis tools
- **History Tracking**: Review and summary of cognitive restructuring journey

### 4. Story House (story-house.html)
- **Three-Stage Writing**:
  - Stage 1: Settling in the Present (emotional fluctuation period)
  - Stage 2: River of Emotions (emotional expression period)
  - Stage 3: New Chapter of the Story (meaning reconstruction period)
- **Safe Writing Environment**: Privacy protection and auto-save
- **Community Sharing**: Anonymous sharing to "Tree of Hope"
- **Writing Guidance**: Stage-based prompts and templates
